Bio

Hey! I am a multidisciplinary artist from Madrid, Spain. My current photographic practice focuses on cities and their impact on people. The city lights, traffic, and crowds are factors that influence the day to day living of citizens. What I try to create are meaningful images that showcase the hectic experience of living in a city.
